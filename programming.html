My programming journey

Beginning of programming

My programming journey began in 2021 when I started using FreeCodeCamp to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My competitive nature and drive to learn kept me hooked on programming. After learning these languages, I decided to study Java intensively and built a project in order to apply for programs at Amazon.

Full stack programming bootcamp

In 2022, I applied for a programming bootcamp sponsored by Amazon, and was accepted by Merit America after expressing my passion for a programming career in the video interview. While working full-time, I dedicated all of my free time to programming for 8 months and completed all assessments and attended all classes. Throughout the program, I demonstrated leadership, dedication, and passion and was chosen to lead a team for the final capstone project.

After graduation

After graduation, I have been actively searching for opportunities to start my career as a software developer and have been taking on freelance work to build websites and gain practical experience in the field.

My background

I grew up in Tokyo, Japan, and ever since I was little, I’ve had a thirst for knowledge and a love for connecting with smart, interesting people. I remember watching Full House as a young kid, which really opened my perspective and sparked a desire to learn more about other cultures and see the world. With a mix of Korean and Osaka roots, I’m definitely not your typical serious Tokyo woman!

Love for learning and new experience

My dad always said that life is richer with a diverse range of experiences, and I couldn’t agree more. So, I took that advice to heart and let my curiosity guide me. That’s how I ended up living in the Netherlands for a few years and traveling to a bunch of incredible places. Eventually, I found myself in sunny California, and I’m loving it here! Living near Silicon Valley really sparked my passion for technology. There's something exciting about seeing Cybertrucks cruising around and imagining a future like Detroit: Become Human. My dream? To live in a world where androids and humans are living, working, and vibing together. I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and staying involved in the tech industry for as long as I can.

My personality

As an INFP, I never thought I’d end up in the tech industry. I was always drawn to the arts and languages when I was younger. I still love listening to music, but my biggest passion now is staying close to the latest technologies. There’s something incredibly rewarding about troubleshooting, debugging, and solving problems—I'm very persistent until I find a solution! I also love creating a harmonious, positive atmosphere in both personal and work environments. :)

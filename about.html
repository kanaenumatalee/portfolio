My background

I grew up in Tokyo, Japan. I have a dominant Japanese background with Korean cultural influences. As a child, I was quiet and introverted, but I always had a love for learning and studying. My interest in other cultures and countries began when I watched the American TV show "Full House." As I watched more foreign films and TV shows, my perspective started to expand. As a teenager, I developed a passion for classical music and taught myself English. After moving to the United States, I decided to pursue a career in programming.

Love for learning and new experience

My father always taught me the importance of having a diverse range of experiences. I took this to heart and followed my curiosity, which led me to live in the Netherlands for a few years and travel to several other countries. Eventually, I ended up settling in the United States. From a young age, I have always been fascinated with learning new things and acquiring new skills. I have always dreamed of having a career that would allow me to continue learning and growing throughout my life.

My personality

I am an INFP and I value self-improvement and living according to my values. I believe in the importance of individuality and strive to create harmonious environments. When my husband started learning programming, I never thought that I could understand such a "mathematical" subject, but my curiosity got the better of me and I wondered if an INFP with an artistic personality could learn programming. I began with FreeCodeCamp and, after a few years, I am now able to create websites.
